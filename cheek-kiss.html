La bise

La bise is the common French greeting involving cheek kisses, a customary gesture of affection and respect among friends, family, and acquaintances, varying in the number of kisses (usually one to four) and region, involving light cheek touches and air kisses rather than actual lip contact. It's used when meeting and leaving, acting as a cultural norm, and can be slightly awkward due to differing regional rules and personal styles, with two kisses (one per cheek) being very common, especially in Paris.
