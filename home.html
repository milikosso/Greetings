Greetings

Gestures like waving or a handshake feel today like the most universal default way to say hello, until you arrive somewhere where there's no contact at all and bowing is the norm. In other parts of the world, people greet each other with a hug or even a cheek kiss. An older way to say hello is a kiss on the hand. And every now and then, you'll run into a fist bump too.
